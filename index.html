DURING COVID-19!

Businesses are shutting down, Ducey's implemented an order to stay home, and the Economic Policy Institute estimates that by July, Arizona's unemployment rate will hit 15.7%.

If we can't work, we can't pay. Collecting rent and mortgages (ALL rent and mortgages--including businesses) during this unprecedented time is immoral, and it should be illegal. Loans aren't the answer. It's time the rest of us get a bail out for a change.

Change happens from the bottom up, not the top down. Join the #PHXRentStrike -- no rent, no mortgage during Covid-19.
